Our History

OVER A DECADE OF GAMING.

Founded in 2015, Sneak's Community started as a single CS:GO Minigames server. Over the years, our community evolved and grown through many games, and we found our true home hosting Counter-Strike: Global Offensive servers, while providing a welcoming environment for all games.

Whether you're grinding Surf, mastering your aim in 1v1 Arenas, or just hanging out in the Chill Zone, this is your community.

Zero Friction

No applications or vetting processes. Jump directly into the action.

Longevity

Over 10 years of continuous operation, we are dedicated to gaming.

Global Reach

Servers in the US with an international community, serving nearly 2 million unique players from over 200 countries and territories.

COMMUNITY RULES

Sneak's Community is built on mutual respect. We strive to maintain a safe and enjoyable environment for everyone.

Community Rules

1

Respect

Be considerate. Friendly banter is normal, but abuse, attacking, or leaking personal information is not tolerated.

5

Staff

Do not undermine administrative activities, impersonate admins, or waste their volunteered time.

2

Spam

No voice/chat spam, including loud microphones, music, prank calls, or talking nonstop.

6

Advertising

No unsolicited advertising; subtle self-promotion via in-game names is permitted.

3

Cheating

No cheating or hacking. We utilize a robust anti-cheat with administrative investigations for a clean gaming experience.

7

Discrimination

Discrimination based on race, gender, or religion is not tolerated and is strictly enforced.

4

Bugs

Do not exploit major, disruptive bugs; their use in community spaces is prohibited.

Timer Rules

1

Account Sharing

No account sharing. Violations result in bans for all accounts and removal of shared times.

2

Alt Accounts

No alt accounts. Populating map/stage/bonus tops with more than one account is prohibited.

3

Exploits

No abusing timer/engine exploits (e.g., super/crouch boosting, trigger hopping, abusing props, looping, etc). Report accidental times to avoid enforcement actions.

See something wrong? File a report on Discord.
